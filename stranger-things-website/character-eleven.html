A young girl with psychokinetic and telepathic abilities who escaped from Hawkins National Laboratory. She was raised in the lab and used as a test subject in experiments.

Brave, loyal, protective, and determined. Despite her traumatic past, she finds a family with Mike, Hopper, and the group.
